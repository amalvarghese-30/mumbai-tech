Building the Future of Industrial Supply

Mumbai-Tech is an innovative startup dedicated to revolutionizing how industries source machinery parts. We combine cutting-edge technology with deep industry expertise to provide efficient, reliable solutions for modern manufacturing challenges.

Our Story

A new vision for industrial procurement

Founded in 2023, Mumbai-Tech emerged from a simple observation: industrial procurement needed to be more efficient, transparent, and reliable. Traditional supply chains were often fragmented, slow, and lacked the technological integration that modern industries demand.

Our founders, with decades of combined experience in industrial engineering and technology, set out to create a platform that would bridge this gap. We started with a clear mission: to build India's most reliable and technologically advanced industrial parts marketplace.

Today, we're building partnerships with manufacturers across the globe while developing our own proprietary technology to streamline the procurement process. We believe that by combining quality products with smart technology, we can help industries operate more efficiently and sustainably.

Our Mission

To revolutionize industrial procurement by providing a seamless, technology-driven platform that connects manufacturers with quality machinery parts, ensuring reliability, transparency, and efficiency in every transaction.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted industrial parts marketplace by 2028, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction while contributing to the growth of India's manufacturing sector.
