Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

What is your minimum order quantity?
We offer flexible MOQs depending on the product and availability. For standard items, MOQ is typically 1 piece. For bulk orders and customized components, please contact our sales team for specific requirements.
What are your delivery timelines?
Standard delivery: 7-14 business days for in-stock items. Express delivery: 3-5 business days (additional charges apply). For custom components: 4-6 weeks depending on complexity and manufacturing requirements.
Do you provide technical specifications and drawings?
Yes, we provide complete technical datasheets, CAD drawings, and 3D models for all our products. These can be downloaded from the product pages or requested from our technical support team.
What is your warranty policy?
We offer a standard 12-month warranty on all products against manufacturing defects. For specific warranty terms and conditions, please refer to our warranty documentation or contact our support team.
Do you accept custom orders?
Yes, we specialize in custom-engineered components. Our technical team can work with you to design and manufacture parts according to your specifications. Contact us with your requirements for a customized solution.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, cheques, and online payments. For corporate clients, we offer credit facilities subject to approval. International payments can be made via wire transfer.
